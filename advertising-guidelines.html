Advertising Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in advertising with MyBusTimes (MBT). To maintain the integrity and focus of our platform, we ask that all advertisers adhere to the following guidelines.

1. Relevance to Transport

All advertisements must be directly related to transportation or mobility. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Public transport services (bus, tram, rail)
  • Fleet management tools or systems
  • Transport apps and tracking tools
  • Tour bus companies or shuttle services
  • Transport-related events or exhibitions

2. Appropriate Content

MBT is accessible to users aged 13 and up. All ads must be family-friendly and suitable for a general audience. We do not allow:


  • Explicit, sexual, or suggestive content
  • Violent, hateful, or discriminatory material
  • Alcohol, tobacco, or recreational drug promotions
  • Political or religious advocacy

3. Visual and Technical Standards

  • Ads must be clear, professional, and of high visual quality
  • No pop-ups, auto-playing sounds, or misleading designs
  • All URLs must direct to secure and relevant websites (HTTPS required)

4. Transparency and Honesty

  • Ads must accurately represent the product or service being offered
  • No misleading claims or deceptive practices
  • Disclose if the ad is sponsored or includes affiliate links

5. Review and Approval

All advertisements are subject to review and approval by the MBT team. We reserve the right to reject or remove any ad that does not meet our standards or align with our values.

6. Contact Information

To inquire about advertising opportunities or submit your content for review, please contact us at advertising@mybustimes.cc.


We appreciate your cooperation in helping keep MyBusTimes a useful and enjoyable resource for the entire transport community.
